Cities I've Lived

  • Niagara Falls, New York
  • Misawa Air Base, Japan
  • Belleville, Illinois
  • Scott Air Force Base, Illinois
  • Mascoutah, Illinois

Niagara Falls, New York

I was born in Niagara Falls. My father was stationed there until I was a year and half. I have no memory of living there, so i would like to visit someday.


Misawa Air Base, Japan

I moved to Misawa Air Base when I was a year and a half. it is located up north of the main island. I moved to different buildings in the base, but I didn't move out of the base for 7 years. I had to move away when I was almost 9 and leaving Misawa was one of the hardest things for me to do.


Belleville, Illinois

My family had to live in Belleville for about a year when my dad was stationed at Scott Air Force Base. We had to wait for housing on base to open up. They were building SWIC across the street when I was living in Belleville. So much has changed since i lived there.


Scott Air Force Base, Illinois

My family lived at Scott Air Force Base for a few years. As I grew up here, I started to like living here. I didn't want to leave the friends that I have made. So my dad decided to retire at Scott in 2005.


Mascoutah, Illinois

After my father retired from the Air Force, we moved to Mascoutah. I was already going to school in Mascoutah, so it made sense for our family to move there. This is the town I still live in.
